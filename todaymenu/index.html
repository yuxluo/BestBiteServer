South Quad

  • 600 East Madison, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109
  • 734-764-0169
  • dining-southquad@umich.edu

Enjoy a tapas style meal at South Quad, where ten mini restaurants are serving up a wide variety of cuisines. Make your own burrito bowl at Sabroso, grab some stir-fry at Two Oceans, or pick up some Mediterranean from Olive Branch — this is just a small fraction of what’s available. Look out for the kosher and halal dining stations as well!

 

Kosher service will be available Sunday through Thursday from 11am – 2pm and 5pm – 8pm and Friday from 11am – 2pm.

Hours of service in observance of Purim

  • Monday, March 9th: 11am-2pm
  • Tuesday, March 10th: 11am-2pm
If you are interested in T0-Go Meal to break your fast on the evening of Monday, March 9th, please submit your request by Friday, March 6.
